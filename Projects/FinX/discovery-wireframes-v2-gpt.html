GPT Product Read

Concept 06 has the most product leverage because it changes discovery from "here are more signals" to "here is the market condition, and here are the signals that fit." That is a clearer promise for FinX, easier to explain in onboarding, and less likely to overwhelm a trader in live market hours.

The GPT improvement is to merge concepts rather than pick one in isolation: Market Pulse as the opening state, Timeline as the audit trail, and Decision Board as an optional filter.

Launch Notes

  • Make every signal show "why shown now": matched state, key metric, and one risk caveat.
  • Keep compliance language factual: pattern observed, outcome tracked, not advice.
  • Add a confidence explanation drawer so users can inspect PCR, VIX, OI flow, and sector inputs.
  • Use outcome tags to build trust over time: confirmed, not confirmed, or inconclusive.
