Annotations
1
View selector — trader declares market view before seeing signals. Sets intent, pre-filters noise, reduces second-guessing.
2
Column headers with counts — all 3 directions always visible. Signal count per column tells trader what options exist if they change their view.
3
Signal mini-card — compact: symbol + type badge + key metric + conviction score bar. Tap to expand to full detail.
4
Conviction score bar — horizontal fill (0–100%) from OI quality + VOL/OI + price confirmation. Green for bull, red for bear signals.
5
Active view confirmation strip — tells trader which view is active and how many matched signals are loaded. Updates when view changes.
Concept 06
Market State Pulse
Don't just show signals — show why they're relevant right now. Read the live market state (PCR, VIX, OI flow, sector rotation), derive a condition, then serve only signals that match that condition. Context before action.
